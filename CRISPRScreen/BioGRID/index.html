How To Read The Scores

Example E3 HUWE1 HECT E3 ligase

Global Importance

This is the percentage of tested BioGRID ORCS screens where the E3 was called a hit. A higher value means the gene is a frequent CRISPR hit across many screen contexts. It can reflect broad biological importance, but also common essentiality.

Phenotype Specificity

This asks whether the hit screens cluster into a small number of BioGRID phenotype labels. High specificity does not mean “more important”; it means the evidence is more concentrated, for example mostly cell proliferation or mostly response to chemicals.

Non-Proliferation Hits

These are HIT=YES screens whose BioGRID phenotype is not cell proliferation. They help surface drug response, viral response, protein accumulation, viability, and other contexts that may be hidden beneath broad essentiality screens.
